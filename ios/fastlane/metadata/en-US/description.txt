Trip Wallet is a smart expense tracker designed for travelers.

KEY FEATURES:

• Multi-Currency Support
Track expenses in 7 major currencies (KRW, USD, EUR, JPY, GBP, AUD, CAD) with automatic conversion.

• Real-Time Exchange Rates
Get up-to-date exchange rates automatically, or set custom rates for your trip.

• Smart Budget Tracking
Set budgets for each trip and monitor your spending with visual progress indicators.

• Expense Categories
Organize expenses into 8 categories: Food, Transportation, Accommodation, Entertainment, Shopping, Activities, Communication, and Miscellaneous.

• Multiple Payment Methods
Track spending across Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, Mobile Payment, and more.

• Visual Statistics
View your spending patterns with beautiful pie charts and bar graphs by category and date.

• Dual Currency Display
See amounts in both original currency and your base currency at a glance.

• Offline First
All data is stored locally on your device. No account required, no data uploaded to servers.

• Bilingual Support
Full support for English and Korean languages.

Start your next trip with Trip Wallet and take control of your travel budget!
